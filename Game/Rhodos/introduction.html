##Aonia:Rhodos##



-Introduction-


Rhodos is part of a trilogy dubbed the Aonia series. The other two parts are called Samos and Lesvos. The main gameplay feature of Rhodos is being able to choose options. Each game has a set of different endings. To unlock Samos, you must reach a certain ending from Rhodos, and to unlock Lesvos, you must reach a certainending from Samos.
